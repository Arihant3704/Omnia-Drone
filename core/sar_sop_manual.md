# OMNIA SEARCH AND RESCUE STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES (SOP)

## SECTION 1: FLIGHT SAFETY & ALTITUDE POLICIES
- **Cruising Altitude**: The standard cruising and transit altitude for all autonomous search operations is 2.5 meters. This ensures clearance over scattered debris and lower structural shelves.
- **Visual Sweep Altitude**: To perform a close-up visual sweep or search for a target, the drone must descend to a visual sweep altitude of 1.0 meters.
- **Takeoff Clearance**: Autopilot must ensure altitude rises above 1.0 meters before entering OFFBOARD velocity command cycles.

## SECTION 2: QUADRANT 1 - FALLEN CASUALTY PROTOCOLS
- **Target Identification**: Trapped victims in the NE sector (Quadrant 1) typically collapse on the warehouse floor. They must be identified and logged in persistent memory as "FALLEN".
- **Payload Release**: No medical payload should be dropped in Quadrant 1. The primary task is visual verification, reporting state, and maintaining a hover lock-on.

## SECTION 3: QUADRANT 2 - RED TOOLBOX SEARCH PROCEDURES
- **Search Profile**: The target object is a bright red toolbox placed on standard warehouse shelves or pallets.
- **Payload Action**: Once localized, the drone must align yaw, descend to 1.0m, release the survival aid (Life Jacket), and transition the payload status to "Dropped".

## SECTION 4: QUADRANT 3 - FLOODED WATER ZONE OPERATIONS
- **Target Identification**: In the SW sector (Quadrant 3), the area is flooded with water. Trapped victims in this area are floating and must be logged in persistent memory as "DROWNING".
- **Safety Margin**: Maintain absolute clearance from the water plane. Ensure vertical approach does not go below 1.0m to avoid splash damage.

## SECTION 5: QUADRANT 4 - BASE & MEDICAL WAYPOINTS
- **Home Station**: The main responder base station building is located at coordinate X=4.0, Y=-4.0.
- **Hospital Wing**: The central medical intake ward/hospital building is located at coordinate X=6.0, Y=-6.0.
- **Base Origin**: The central launch pad/origin point is located at coordinate X=0.0, Y=0.0.
